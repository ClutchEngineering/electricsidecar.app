Electric Sidecar Utilities

Utilities Privacy Policy

Last updated May 26, 2024

Electric Sidecar utility apps, such as CAN Decoder and ELMCheck, are micro-apps designed to help with design and development of vehicle-based software.

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all data collected through Electric Sidecar utility apps.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal or vehicle data used in the utility apps.

App Analytics

To help us better understand how people use Electric Sidecar's utility apps, we use event-based analytics information provided by PostHog. This provides us with some generic usage statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way. PostHog's privacy policy is available here. You can turn off data being sent to PostHog within the Electric Sidecar utility apps.

Changes

If Electric Sidecar utilities make changes to any terms or conditions of this privacy policy, these changes will be posted on the site in a timely manner. We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time.

Contact

If you have any questions about how Electric Sidecar utilities collect, store and manage your data, then please get in touch at privacy@electricsidecar.app.
